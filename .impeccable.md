## Design Context

### Users
Casual buyers and sellers in a local marketplace (Kuwait-based, Q84Sale/4Sale). They're browsing listings, looking for new cars and new cars details — often on mobile, often in quick sessions. The job to be done: find what you're looking for fast.

### Brand Personality
**Premium, Sleek, Confident.** Q84Sale positions itself above typical classifieds — it's a modern marketplace that feels trustworthy and high-end while remaining accessible to everyday users.

### Emotional Goals
The interface should evoke all four pillars simultaneously:
- **Trust & confidence** — Users must feel safe transacting. Visual stability, consistent patterns, and clear information hierarchy reinforce reliability.
- **Speed & efficiency** — Zero friction. Every interaction should feel fast and purposeful. No unnecessary steps or decorative bloat.
- **Excitement & discovery** — The thrill of finding a great deal. Subtle energy in promotions and featured listings without overwhelming the core experience.
- **Calm & simplicity** — Clean layouts, generous whitespace, and a restrained palette keep cognitive load low despite rich content.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Flat, modern, blue-dominant. Solid colors, no gradients. Minimal borders, no heavy shadows.
- **Motion**: Functional only — slide-in transitions (`translateY`), shimmer loading, `ease-in-out` timing. Nothing bouncy or decorative.
- **Typography**: Single custom font ("sakr font"), bold type scale (32/24/16/14), weight-driven hierarchy (400/500/700).
- **Theme**: Light mode with white backgrounds and `--neutral_50` sections.
- **Anti-references**: Cluttered classifieds (Craigslist-style), gradient-heavy apps, bouncy/playful animation styles, multi-font designs.

### Design Principles
1. **Token-faithful** — Every color, spacing, and typographic choice must come from the design token system. No ad-hoc values.
2. **Motion with purpose** — Animations communicate state changes only. Slide-in from edges, never fade in place. Respect the timing scale (0.2s micro, 0.3s CTA, 0.5s content).
3. **Flat confidence** — No shadows, no gradients, no rounded-everything. The brand blue (`#0062ff`) anchors every interactive surface. Cards are flat with subtle borders.
4. **Content-first density** — Let listings and content drive layout. Minimal chrome, no decorative elements. Whitespace is intentional, not filler.
5. **Accessible by default** — WCAG AA compliance minimum. High-contrast text (`--neutral_900` on white), visible focus states for keyboard users, respect for reduced motion preferences.
